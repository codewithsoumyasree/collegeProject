Frequently Asked Questions (Admin)

To log in to the Admin Panel, use the credentials created in the database. Enter your username and password to access the dashboard.

The "Forgot Password" option is not available, as admin credentials are directly managed and created in the database. Please contact your database administrator if you need assistance with resetting your password.

Admin credentials can be updated directly in the database. To change your username or password, contact the system administrator to update your details in the database.

To ensure the security of your account, use strong passwords that are unique and difficult to guess. Additionally, it's recommended to store your credentials securely and to avoid sharing them with others.

The ability to add or remove other admin users depends on your privileges. If you have sufficient permissions, you can modify user roles through the database or via an admin management interface provided in the system.

If your account is compromised, immediately contact the system administrator to reset your credentials in the database. Ensure that a new, strong password is set to protect the account.
